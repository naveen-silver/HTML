Anime

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, and dreamt of being a pirate since childhood from the influence of his idol and mentor Red-Haired Shanks. At the age of 17, Luffy sets sail from the East Blue Sea to the Grand Line in search of the legendary treasure One Piece, to succeed Gol D. Roger as "King of the Pirates". He fights multiple antagonists, and aids and befriends the inhabitants of several islands on his journey. Usually cheerful, he becomes serious and even aggressive when he fights. Luffy uses his rubber body to concentrate his power, executing a range of attacks. In his signature attack, the Gum-Gum Pistol, he slingshots punches at opponents from a distance

Satoru Gojo

Satoru Gojo is a character from the anime and manga series Jujutsu Kaisen, created by Gege Akutami. He is a powerful jujutsu sorcerer and serves as a teacher at the Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical School. Gojo is known for his laid-back personality, exceptional combat skills, and his iconic blindfold, which he wears to seal his immense power. He plays a crucial role in the series, guiding the main protagonist, Yuji Itadori, and his friends as they battle curses and uncover the mysteries of the jujutsu world.

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake is a fictional character from the anime and manga series Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a highly skilled ninja and serves as a mentor to the main characters, Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno. Known for his laid-back attitude, silver hair, and the Sharingan eye he covers with a mask, Kakashi is renowned for his intelligence, combat prowess, and mastery of various jutsu techniques. His backstory reveals a tragic past that shapes his character and relationships throughout the series.

Izuku Midoriya

Izuku Midoriya is the main protagonist of the anime and manga series My Hero Academia, created by Kohei Horikoshi. He is a young boy born without superpowers in a world where they are the norm, but he still dreams of becoming a hero. After a chance encounter with his idol All Might, the world's greatest hero, he inherits All Might's Quirk, One For All, and enrolls in U.A. High School to train to become a professional hero.

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha is a character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. Itachi is the older brother of Sasuke Uchiha, and is responsible for killing all the members of their clan, sparing only Sasuke. He appears working as a terrorist from the organisation Akatsuki and serves as Sasuke's greatest enemy.

Gaara

Gaara is a character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is the jinchuriki of the One-Tail, Shukaku, and initially serves as an antagonist in the series. Gaara is known for his sand manipulation abilities and his tragic backstory, which leads him to seek recognition and acceptance from others. Over time, he becomes a more complex character and ultimately allies with Naruto and the other protagonists.

Kajiu No. 8

Kajiu No. 8 is a character from the manga and anime series created by Naoya Matsumoto. The story follows Kafka Hibino, a man in his 30s who dreams of joining the Defense Force to fight against Kaiju, giant monsters that threaten humanity. After an unexpected encounter, he gains the ability to transform into a Kaiju himself, leading to a unique perspective on the battle between humans and monsters.

Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi Fushiguro is a character from the anime and manga series Jujutsu Kaisen, created by Gege Akutami. He is a student at Jujutsu High and a member of the Zenin clan. Megumi is known for his serious demeanor and strong sense of justice. He possesses the Ten Shadows Technique, which allows him to summon shikigami to fight on his behalf. Throughout the series, he grapples with his family's expectations and his own beliefs about strength and responsibility.

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is the main protagonist of the anime and manga series Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a young ninja with dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. Naruto is known for his determination, optimism, and the ability to form strong bonds with others. Throughout the series, he faces various challenges and adversaries while striving to protect his friends and achieve his goals.

Ryomen Sukuna

Ryomen Sukuna is a major antagonist in the anime and manga series Jujutsu Kaisen, created by Gege Akutami. He is a powerful Cursed Spirit and is known as the King of Curses. Sukuna was once a human sorcerer who became a curse after his death. He possesses immense strength, speed, and cursed energy, making him a formidable opponent. Throughout the series, he often clashes with the protagonists, particularly Yuji Itadori, who becomes his unwilling host.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is a prominent character in the anime and manga series Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a member of the Uchiha clan and is known for his exceptional skills as a ninja. Initially introduced as a rival to Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke's character is defined by his quest for power and revenge against his brother, Itachi Uchiha, who massacred their clan. Throughout the series, Sasuke undergoes significant development, grappling with his motivations and ultimately seeking redemption.

Shoto Todoroki

Shoto Todoroki is a character from the anime and manga series My Hero Academia, created by Kohei Horikoshi. He is a student at U.A. High School and is known for his dual Quirk, which allows him to control both ice and fire. Shoto is the son of Endeavor, the No. 1 hero, and has a complex relationship with his father due to their troubled past. Throughout the series, he struggles with his identity and strives to become a hero while reconciling with his family history.
